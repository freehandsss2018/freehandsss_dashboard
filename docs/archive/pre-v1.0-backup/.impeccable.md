# Freehandsss Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
*   **Primary User (Ling Au)**: Business owner and sales representative. She uses the Dashboard on an iPad/iPhone during customer consultations to generate quotes and process orders. Needs extreme simplicity, large touch targets, and "idiot-proof" flows.
*   **Administrator (Fat Mo)**: System architect and developer. He uses the Dashboard on a desktop to audit finances, manage the product database, and troubleshoot system errors. Needs high data density and decision-support tools.

### Brand Personality
*   **Premium**: Reflects the high-end nature of bespoke baby keepsakes.
*   **Trustworthy**: Handling precious memories and significant financial transactions.
*   **Nostalgic/Warm**: Celebrates the growth of children.
*   **3-Word Personality**: Elegant, Reliable, Human.

### Aesthetic Direction
*   **Visual Tone**: Modern Glassmorphism with a warm, organic color palette (Tan, Gold, Soft Grays).
*   **References**: Apple-style "Clean & Glassy" interfaces, high-end jewelry or baby boutique branding.
*   **Theme**: Optimized for Light Mode (warm, bright, trustworthy) with a desktop-focused "Data Cockpit" and a mobile-focused "POS Wizard".

### Design Principles
1.  **Frictionless Entry**: Minimizing typing on mobile; maximizing selection and automation.
2.  **Visual Truth**: Financial data must be clear, unambiguous, and mapped to the "Product Bible".
3.  **Hierarchy of Action**: Primary actions (like "Sync to Database") must be physically impossible to miss.
4.  **Resilience**: UI must gracefully handle complex product variants (5D Lookup Logic) without overwhelming the user.
